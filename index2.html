How IdeasHub Works — Quick overview

1. Post

Anyone can post an idea: research, project, startup plan or a simple thought that can be useful for others.

2. Discover

Search or browse ideas by tag, SDG or region.

3. Request Collaboration

Send a structured request to the idea owner. If approved, a collaboration space is created.

4. Credit & Benefits

Originator gets visible credit, co-authorship and can choose licensing or benefit-sharing options.
